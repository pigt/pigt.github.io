Rules

For this experiment, you will be given four decks of cards: A, B, C, and D.

You need to select one card at a time from any deck of your choice.

Each time you select a card, you are going to win some credit.

Every so often, you are also going to lose some. It can either be more or less than the credit you win.

You are absolutely free to switch from one deck to the other at any time, and as often as you wish.

The goal of the game is to win as much credit as possible and avoid losing as much as possible.

As a headstart, you are being given £2000 of credit.

Keep in mind that out of these four decks, some are worse than others. And to win, you should try to stay away from bad decks.

Also note that the computer does not change the order of the cards once the game begins. It does not make you lose at random, or make you lose credit based on the last card you picked.
